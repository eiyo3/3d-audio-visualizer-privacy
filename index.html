About This Extension

3D Audio Visualizer Pro is a Chrome browser extension that transforms any audio into stunning real-time 3D visuals powered by WebGL and Three.js. Connect a microphone or upload a local audio file and watch your music come to life.

  • 🌐 3D Sphere — a wireframe sphere that pulses and rotates to the beat
  • 📊 Bar Graph — classic frequency bars rendered in 3D space
  • ✨ Particles — hundreds of particles that react to audio energy in real time
  • 🎤 Live microphone input support
  • 📁 Upload and visualize local audio files (MP3, WAV, OGG, and more)
  • 🎬 Record and export your visualizations as video
