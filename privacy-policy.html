1 Overview

3D Audio Visualizer Pro ("the Extension") is a Chrome browser extension that renders real-time 3D audio visualizations on web pages, including YouTube. This Privacy Policy explains what information is (and is not) collected when you use the Extension.

Short version: We do not collect any personal information. The Extension operates entirely within your browser and does not send any data to our servers.

2 Data We Do Not Collect

The Extension does not collect, store, transmit, or share any of the following:

  • Personal identification information (name, email address, etc.)
  • Browsing history or URLs visited
  • Audio content or media data from pages you visit
  • Device information or hardware identifiers
  • Location data
  • Usage analytics or crash reports
  • Cookies or local storage data beyond what the browser itself manages
All audio processing is performed locally in your browser using the Web Audio API. No audio data ever leaves your device.

3 Why Certain Permissions Are Requested

The Extension may request the following browser permissions, used solely for the stated purposes:

  • activeTab / scripting — Injects the visualizer canvas into the current browser tab so the visualization can be displayed on screen.
  • storage — Saves your visual preferences (e.g., theme, color settings) locally in Chrome's built-in storage. This data never leaves your device.
No permission is used to read page content, capture audio streams for transmission, or access any sensitive user data.

4 Payments & Billing (ExtensionPay / Stripe)

3D Audio Visualizer Pro offers an optional paid upgrade ("Pro" tier) processed through ExtensionPay, a third-party payment service for Chrome extensions. ExtensionPay uses Stripe as its underlying payment processor.

We never see or store your payment information. All credit card numbers, billing addresses, and financial details are entered directly on Stripe's secure servers and are governed solely by Stripe's Privacy Policy.

When you subscribe to the Pro tier:

  • You are redirected to a secure Stripe-hosted payment page managed by ExtensionPay.
  • Your payment details are transmitted directly to Stripe — never to our servers.
  • ExtensionPay returns only a boolean "paid / not paid" status to the Extension in order to unlock Pro features. No financial data is shared with us.
  • Stripe may collect and process data as described in their own Privacy Policy.
